# Coin Collecting Hub: The Architecture of Numismatics

Welcome to the **Coin Collecting Hub**, Wikantik's central index for the study and investment of currency. In 2026, numismatics has evolved from a niche historical hobby into a sophisticated **$18.1 billion global asset class**, bridging the gap between [economic history](EconomicHistory), [fine art](ArtHistory), and [materials science](Metallurgy).
