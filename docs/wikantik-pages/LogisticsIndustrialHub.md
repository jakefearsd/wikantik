# Logistics and Industrial Systems Hub

This hub anchors the wiki's content related to the physical movement of goods and the optimization of manufacturing processes. It serves as the practical application layer for the pure mathematics found in the Operations Research cluster.

## Ⅰ. Manufacturing and Production
Optimizing the factory floor.
*   [Lean Manufacturing Principles Hub](LeanManufacturingPrinciplesHub) — The philosophical and systematic approach to waste reduction (Toyota Production System).
*   [Manufacturing Sequencing](ManufacturingSequencing) — The math of the Job Shop Scheduling Problem (JSSP) and semiconductor fab optimization.

## Ⅱ. Warehouse and Distribution
Managing storage and retrieval at scale.
*   [Warehouse Automation Hub](WarehouseAutomationHub) — The overarching index for modern fulfillment centers.
*   [Warehouse Picking Optimization](WarehousePickingOptimization) — Picker routing heuristics (S-Shape) and Amazon's Goods-to-Person robotics.
*   [Warehouse Slotting Optimization](WarehouseSlottingOptimization) — Center of Gravity and Golden Zone mathematics.

## Ⅲ. Supply Chain and Transport
Moving goods across the globe.
*   [Supply Chain and Logistics Optimization](SupplyChainAndLogisticsOptimization) — Network design and the Bullwhip Effect.
*   [Vehicle Routing Problem](VehicleRoutingProblem) — The computational challenge of last-mile delivery.
