### NavalHistory
**NavalHistory** refers to the study of Portugal's maritime activities, naval conflicts, and shipbuilding advancements from the 13th to the 21st century. It highlights the nation's pivotal role in global exploration, trade, and colonial expansion through its powerful navy and innovative maritime technology.  

Portugal's naval history is deeply intertwined with its rise as a global maritime power during the Age of Discovery, marked by voyages led by figures like Vasco da Gama and the development of ships such as the caravel. This history also encompasses naval wars, treaties, and the evolution of Portugal's maritime influence in the Atlantic, Indian Ocean, and beyond.
