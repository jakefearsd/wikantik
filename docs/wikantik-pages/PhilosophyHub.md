# Philosophy Hub

Philosophy provides the foundational frameworks for understanding existence, knowledge, ethics, and reason. This hub organizes Wikantik's philosophical content, ranging from classical branches like Metaphysics to modern inquiries into the Philosophy of Mind and AI ethics.

- [Metaphysics](Metaphysics) — The nature of reality, existence, and the fundamental categories of being
- [Epistemology](Epistemology) — The study of knowledge, belief, and the justification of truth
- [Phenomenology](Phenomenology) — The philosophical study of the structures of experience and consciousness

## Logic and Science
