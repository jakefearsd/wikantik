Why Wikantik

Four things your current wiki doesn't do

📝

Markdown in, freedom out

Plain markdown, git-backed, self-hostable. Your knowledge is yours — export it, diff it, walk away with it. No lock-in, no hostage situation.

🎯

Search that actually finds it

Hybrid retrieval — keyword + meaning + a knowledge-graph rerank. Ask in plain language, get the right page. Not keyword roulette.

🤖

Your AI's favorite coworker

Native MCP servers mean your agents query, retrieve, and cite your wiki directly. The knowledge graph hands them entities and relationships, not a wall of HTML.

👥

Built for two readers

Every page serves humans (clean, fast reading) and machines (token-budgeted projections, runbooks, verification metadata). Write once; both get the good version.
